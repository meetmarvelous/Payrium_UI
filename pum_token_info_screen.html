About PUM

Payrium Token (PUM) is the heartbeat of the Payrium ecosystem. It functions as a utility token designed to reduce transaction friction and reward active participants. By holding PUM, users unlock significantly lower trading fees and gain access to premium features within the Payrium wallet.

Furthermore, PUM serves as a governance instrument, allowing the community to vote on protocol upgrades and treasury allocations, ensuring a decentralized future for the platform.
